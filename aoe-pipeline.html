Pipeline

Comprehensive capabilities for the transportation of Oil & Natural Gas through Cross Country Pipeline, Trunk & Spur Lines. Capabilities include engineering of compressor stations, metering & regulating stations and other associated facilities.

  • Dispatch & Receiving Facilities

Eurasia

Eurasia Consulting LLC (ECE), established in 2014, is a multi-disciplinary engineering company that provides Engineering services from concept to commissioning in the Hydrocarbon sector in UAE and the Middle East. ECE is a Triune Group Company with Triune Energy Services Pvt. Ltd (TES) as its technical partner. TES is one of the largest Indian owned private sector engineering companies.
